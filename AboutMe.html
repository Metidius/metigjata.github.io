About Me

Hello!

I'm a Computer Science graduate located in Central Manchester currently seeking for a career in Game Development.

My main focus in my game developing projects is manifesting what others love in other games that keep them hooked. I follow a psychologist by the name of Nick Yee who specialises in motivation behind Video Games and has found that Achievement, Rules & System and Social Competition are the main 3 factors that motivate users to continuinly play the game.

I have 2 years of experience using Unity as well as Blender. I have a great passion for Art and Graphics, as I aim to completely design everything in my game from scratch for not only originality but to use my skill.
